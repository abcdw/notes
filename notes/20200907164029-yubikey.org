#+title: YubiKey

A security device for different purposes:
- storing [[id:9353387c-bc3c-4c2f-8882-7ae5500bb9ca][pgp]] keys
- generating one-time passwords
- doing 2 factor authentication

Also can be referenced as a smartcard.
