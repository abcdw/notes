#+title: Training More Effectively

[[https://youtu.be/Z_wSBtIGgPs][10 Science-Based Ways to Get Stronger in Half the Time]]

- Do fingeboarding at the beginning of the session.
- Do exercises faster :: more strength gains, 3-5 reps per set, 7-9/10
  effort (RPE), sometimes do exercise to failure to learn the max.
- Do supersets :: same gains, better endurance and less time spent.
  Preferably pull+push.
