#+title: Theming Days

The idea from https://youtu.be/HZWKckdo2UU to color days in specific
themes to batch related task and simplify planning/scheduling
activities:

- Monday :: FOSS maintanance
- Tuesday :: beweekly planning/streaming
- Thursday :: research.

Changing locations for particular activities can be benifitial as
well.
