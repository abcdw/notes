#+title: Geyikbairi Cookies Recipe

- 2.5 cup of flour.
- 1 cup of sugar.
- 5 gr vanillin.
- 10 gr baking powder/bicarb.
- 2 eggs.
- 150g butter.
- 100g chocolate.
- 100g dried cranberry.

Melt the butter, mix everything else and add it into butter slowly.

Bake at 180 degrees for 15 minutes.
