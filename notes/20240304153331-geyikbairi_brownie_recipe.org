#+title: Geyikbairi Brownie Recipe

- 180g Butter.
- 180g Chocolate.
Melt it.

Separate:
- 3 eggs.
- 200g sugar.
Mix.

Add melted, but not hot butter to it.

Add 80g of flour with a pinch of baking powder to it.

Bake at 160 degrees for 25 minutes.
