#+title: ed25519

Ed25519 is a public-key signature system with several attractive features.

It looks interesting to me because it should be more performant than
rsa-4096, without compromising security.  I currently suffer a bit
from [[id:3efb3781-5efa-4037-8a95-abeec91292f4][YubiKey]] + RSA-4096 performance.
