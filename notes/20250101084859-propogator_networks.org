#+title: Propogator Networks

Graph of interconnected cells, which contains information about values
and can reactively update themselves depending on new input.

[[id:5077b101-e79d-4a02-bcba-6839d03b0b92][Functional Reactive Programming]] can be built on top of propogators.

Propogation can be a fundamental computing principle and even
substitute the evaluation of expressions.
